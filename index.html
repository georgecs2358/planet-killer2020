PlanetKiller 2020

BETA

The Empire has finished development of the Death Star. As leader of the fledgling Rebel Alliance, your only hope is to find the 3 secret plans hidden across the Empire, which will reveal how to destroy this deadly weapon before the galaxy is layed to ruin. Can you outwit the devious Grand Moff Tarkin to save the Alliance from destruction?

How to Play

  1. Deploy your fleets across the 9 Rebel planets. Do this using the allocation buttons towards the bottom of the display.
  2. Attack the Empire by first clicking a Rebel planet, then the Empire planet to attack. The more attacking vs defending fleets, the greater chance of defeating the Empire.
  3. Press 'End Turn' when finished. Now the Empire will begin their turn...

Tips

  • Secret plans are hidden on some Empire planets, so make sure to attack atleast one planet per turn.
  • At the end of each turn credits are used to build rebel fleets; protect planets which generate lots of credits per turn.
  • Food per turn is used to supply fleets, hence running low will cause attrition.
  • Due to focusing all resources on the Death Star, the Empire only invades planets which are not defended. Try and maintain 2 fleets on each planet.
